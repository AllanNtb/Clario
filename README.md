# Clario

Clario is a modern **web-based life admin dashboard** designed to help users manage their **appointments, bills, subscriptions, and important tasks** in one convenient place. With Clario, users can plan their personal and business lives efficiently, receive reminders, and track recurring items—all from their browser or as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

---

## **Features**

- **Personal & Business Dashboards**: Keep your personal and professional tasks organized separately.  
- **Upcoming Items**: View upcoming appointments, bills, and events.  
- **Recurring Items**: Easily track monthly bills or weekly appointments.  
- **Add/Edit/Delete Items**: Quickly manage tasks with a user-friendly interface.  
- **PWA Ready**: Install Clario on desktop or mobile for offline access and instant availability.  
- **Notifications**: Reminders for upcoming tasks and bills (browser notifications).  
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on **desktop, tablet, and mobile devices**.  
- **Theming**: Toggle between light and dark mode.
